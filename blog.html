My Journey Learning HTML and CSS From Scratch

Starting my journey with HTML and CSS from scratch was intimidating at first, but it quickly became an exciting puzzle of turning code into visual reality. I spent countless hours practicing the basics, from structuring simple layouts to fine-tuning styles until every element sat exactly where I wanted it. Seeing my first handwritten page come to life on a browser made me realize that I didn't just want to browse the web—I wanted to build it.

Why I Chose Web Design as My Path

I chose web design because it lets me combine my creative side with my love for technology in a way that actually helps people. I really enjoy the challenge of taking a messy idea and turning it into a clean, easy-to-use website that looks great. It’s rewarding to know that the work I do can make someone’s digital experience a lot smoother and more enjoyable.
